1. About this policy

PKD-ProBase is a WhatsApp-native professional directory operated by PKD for a closed community of vetted professionals. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you interact with the PKD-ProBase WhatsApp bot, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the rights you have over it under Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).

If you disagree with any part of this policy, please do not use the PKD-ProBase service. You can decline consent the first time you message the bot, in which case no personal data about you will be stored.

4. Lawful basis for processing

Under Act 843, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent — obtained explicitly the first time you interact with the bot. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interest — for fraud prevention, moderation of content, and maintaining community trust.
  • Legal obligation — for retention of audit logs and consent records as required by Ghanaian law.

5. How consent works

The first time you message PKD-ProBase, the bot will show you a consent notice summarising this policy and ask you to reply YES or NO. No personal information about you is stored until you reply YES. If you reply NO, the bot will say goodbye and no profile or record about you will be created.

We keep a snapshot of the exact consent text you agreed to, so that if our consent language changes later we can always prove which version you consented to. You may be asked to re-consent if the consent text changes materially.

8. Your rights under Ghana's Data Protection Act

Act 843 gives you the following rights. You can exercise all of them directly through the PKD-ProBase bot:

  • Right of access — type /my-data to the bot. It will return a summary of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — you can update your business listings and profile details in the bot. For name corrections, message us via the contact above.
  • Right to deletion / erasure — type /delete-my-data to the bot. After a confirmation step, your personal information will be anonymised and your listings will be deactivated. Audit logs will be retained in anonymised form where required by law.
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdrawing consent triggers the same effect as /delete-my-data.
  • Right to object — if you object to a specific use of your data, message us and a moderator will review.
  • Right to complain — you may lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission of Ghana at https://dpc.gov.gh.

9. How we protect your data

  • All messages to and from the bot travel over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Incoming webhooks are verified using HMAC-SHA256 signatures to prevent spoofing.
  • Our database is accessible only from the application server. There is no public database endpoint.
  • Secrets and API tokens are held in environment variables, not in source code.
  • Personal data (phone numbers, names, company registration numbers) is never written into application logs. Logs use internal identifiers only.
  • Company registration numbers are stored for verification only. They are never returned in search results or shown to other users.
  • Admin commands are restricted to a pre-registered list of authorised numbers.

10. Minimum age

PKD-ProBase is a professional directory for adult professionals. By consenting to use the bot, you confirm that you are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. International data transfers

Our hosting provider may operate servers outside Ghana. Where this occurs, we rely on data processing agreements and appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected to the standard required by Act 843. By consenting to use the bot, you agree to this cross-border transfer.

12. Data breaches

If we become aware of a personal data breach that is likely to result in harm to you, we will notify you via WhatsApp and notify the Data Protection Commission of Ghana as soon as practicable, in line with our obligations under Act 843.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes that affect your consent will trigger a re-consent prompt from the bot the next time you interact with it. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always shows when the policy was last revised.
